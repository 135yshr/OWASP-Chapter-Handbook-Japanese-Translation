==Spend any chapter funds in accordance with the OWASP goals, code of ethics, and principles==Chapter funds should be used for your chapter and must be spent in line with the OWASP Foundation goals, principles, and code of ethics. Accordingly, chapter finances should be handled in a transparent manner. For more suggestions on handling chapter funds, see [[Chapter Handbook/Chapter_4:_Chapter_Administration#Handling_Money|section 4.7 on Handling Money]].˜a–ó‚Í‚±‚Ì‰º‚É‘‚«‘«‚µ‚Ä‰º‚³‚¢
