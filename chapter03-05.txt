==Student, University, or Academic Chapters==OWASP student, university, academic chapters must follow the same mandatory guidelines set for standard OWASP Chapters, however there are generally special funds and people in place to provide support and guidance for these chapters. Visit the [https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Student_Chapters_Program Student Chapters] and [https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Academic_Supporter Academic Supporter] pages for details on these guidelines.
