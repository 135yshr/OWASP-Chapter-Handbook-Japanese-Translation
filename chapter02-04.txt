==Give official meeting notice through the wiki, chapter mailing list, and OWASP Calendar==Chapter meetings must be posted to the OWASP wiki (on the chapterfs page) and a meeting announcement must be sent out to the OWASP mailing list (i.e. owasp-CHAPTERNAME@lists.owasp.org) to notify the OWASP community of each upcoming meeting. Also, the meeting must be listed on the OWASP Global Events Calendar.˜a–ó‚Í‚±‚Ì‰º‚É‘‚«‘«‚µ‚Ä‰º‚³‚¢
